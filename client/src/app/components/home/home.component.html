About Raidy Check

Raidy Check is design to help you manage your group. You will be able to compare characters side by side to see who is ready and who is not. You may set iLvl caps to see which characters (or individual pieces of gear) are ready to fight along side you. Create multiple groups for your mythic dungeons or your different guild runs.

You may also see groups that other players have set up. You will be able to favorite these groups so that you don't need to search for them again. Go ahead and share the groups that you have created and showoff how awesome your group is doing.
